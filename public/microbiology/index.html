Microbiology

Microbiology is the study of micro-organisms which can be sub-divided into:

  1. Bacteriology: study of bacteria (single: bacterium)
  2. Mycology: study of fungus (single: fungi)
  3. Virology: study of virus
  4. Parasitology: study of parasites

Although both virus and parasite are not considered micro-organisms to be precise, they are commonly grouped into the field of medical microbiology due to their clinical significance.

Although we will not discuss about Imunology (study of immune system) and protistology (study of protist) they are also commonly grouped into the field of microbiology as well.

In this section we will focus on microbiology topics that are clinically significant.
